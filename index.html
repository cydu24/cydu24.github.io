Research Interests

I am dedicated to advancing agentic AI systems with a focus on:

  • Interleaved Agent: Enhance the model's capability in handling long-horizon tasks for complex agents and the generalization performance of agents, ultimately achieving agent-in-the-loop self-iteration.
  • World-Play Agent: Developing expansive, autonomously operating open-world World-play. This extends towards the exploration of its interactions with personalized characters and real environments.
